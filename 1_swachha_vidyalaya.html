"Swachha Vidyalaya Abhiyan"

Our CSR project "Swachha Vidyalaya Abhiyan" of constructing toilet in school at Khamgaon was planned in two phases - 1. The construction and 2. Training the students on hygiene maintenance.

Both the phases have been completed now and the students have started using the same happily.

Phase 1 - The construction

First phase has been completed and the toilets have been handed over to the school. As shown below, earlier there was only 1 toilet for girls without door & 2 urinals which were not in good condition, the walls were shaky along with broken floor and an irregular water supply. To improve these deplorable and unhygienic conditions, we have provided 4 units for boys consisting of 3 urinals & 1 toilet and 3 regular toilets for girls. Also, as the girls in 1st standard are shorter in height, they face inconvenience while using the normal toilet so we have provided 1 more toilet which is better accessible to them.

The privacy has been maintained through the outer panels and separate entries, water taps are provided inside each toilet, separate wash basins have also been provided, and in case of water scarcity, they can use the water stored in the tank.

Phase 2

In phase 2, we had organised a program at school where FFPL employees had trained students on how to keep the toilets clean and importance of hygiene maintenance.

We have also designed a maintenance agreement in which school has appointed a person who will be cleaning the toilets and our selected employees will ensure audit of these toilets for 1 to 2 years.
